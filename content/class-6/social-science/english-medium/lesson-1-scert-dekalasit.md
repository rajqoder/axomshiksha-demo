## Exercise 1

### 1. Answer the following questions:

**(a) What is the name of the solar family?**

**Answer:** The name of the solar family is the **Solar System**.

**(b) Name the satellite of the Earth.**

**Answer:** The name of the Earth's satellite is the **Moon**.

### 2. List the names of the planets in the solar system in order of their distance from the sun.

**Answer:**
1. Mercury
2. Venus
3. Earth
4. Mars
5. Jupiter
6. Saturn
7. Uranus
8. Neptune

### 3. Although the Moon is much smaller than the Sun, why do both appear equal in size from the Earth?

**Answer:** Although the Moon is much smaller than the Sun, it is much closer to the Earth. That is why the Moon and the Sun appear to be of the same size from the Earth.

## Exercise 2

### 1. Answer the following questions:

**(a) What is a miniature model of the large earth?**

**Answer:** A globe is a miniature model of the large earth.

**(b) What is the circumference of the Earth?**

**Answer:** The circumference of the Earth is approximately 40,000 km.

**(c) In which direction does the Earth rotate?**

**Answer:** The Earth rotates from west to east.

**(d) What is the Earth's axis?**

**Answer:** The imaginary line passing through the center of the Earth connecting the two poles (North Pole and South Pole) is called the Earth's axis.
